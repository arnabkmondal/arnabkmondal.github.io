I have received my Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Telecommunication from Jadavpur University, India in 2013. Right after my graduation, I joined Centre for Development of Telematics (C-DOT), Delhi, and served as a research engineer there until July 2018. In C-DOT I had the opportunity to participate in cutting-edge projects such as the Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing (DWDM) and Packet Optical Transport Platform (P-OTP). I joined IIT Delhi as a Ph.D. scholar in July, 2018 under the guidance of Prof. Prathosh AP and Prof. Parag Singla. My research interests lie primarily within the field of deep generative models and applied deep learning. My Ph.D. was supported by the Prime Minister's Research Fellows (PMRF) Scheme by Govt. of India. Currently, I am working as a senior researcher in the graph AI team of Fujitsu Research India Private Limited.
